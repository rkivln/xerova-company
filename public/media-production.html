What formats do you deliver the final files in?
+
We deliver video in MP4 (H.264 & H.265), MOV, and ProRes formats depending on your needs — optimised for web, broadcast, or social platforms. Photos are delivered as high-resolution JPEGs and TIFFs, with RAW files available on Studio and Enterprise packages.
How long does a typical project take from shoot to delivery?
+
Turnaround depends on the package — Spark projects are delivered in 5 business days, Studio in 3 days, and Enterprise with priority 48-hour delivery. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee on all packages.
Do you travel for shoots outside your city?
+
Absolutely. We've shot across India and internationally. Travel, accommodation, and logistics costs are billed separately and quoted upfront — no hidden fees. Our team is fully mobile and self-sufficient for multi-day travel shoots.
Can I approve the content before final delivery?
+
Yes — all projects include revision rounds where you can review rough cuts and provide feedback before final delivery. We use Frame.io for seamless video review with timestamped comments. Your approval is required before any final file is exported.
Do you provide the crew and equipment, or do I need to arrange anything?
+
We handle everything — camera operators, lighting technicians, drone pilots, sound recordists, and all equipment. You just need to provide the location access and any brand assets (products, talent briefings, etc.). We take care of the rest.
What's your payment structure?
+
We require a 50% advance to confirm your booking and begin pre-production. The remaining 50% is due upon final delivery. For Enterprise packages, milestone-based payment schedules are available. We accept bank transfer, UPI, and international wire transfers.
